Harman International: New York City
The line between retail store and performance space just got a bit blurrier (and a whole lot funkier) with the opening of the Shawmut-built 8,500 SF Harman International flagship on Madison Avenue.
Working with an aggressive 10-week schedule, Project Executive Kevin Piraino, Project Manager Nick Meyer, Superintendent Jeff Borst, Construction Manager Bob Goemaat, Assistant Project Manager Sophie Wilkinson and the entire team first completed demo of the space and then performed a design/build renovation in partnership with Gensler. The transformation included an interactive DJ console, a first-floor stage area where Harman can host recording artists, and the Sound Spider, an interactive table display and headphone rack where shoppers will be able to test a full range of Harman headphones hanging from the ceiling.
To conceal the extensive wiring needed throughout the store, our team used a false floor on the first level. Hanging clouds (procured from Germany) were also installed to enhance acoustics while soundproof doors were set up in the basement’s JBL Synthesis Home Theater Room.
Despite the huge undertaking, the team managed to produce both a high-end showroom and an interactive experience center for customers that blended together in perfect harmony—even though no one on our crew can hold a tune!

January was Mentor Month, but really, every month should be.
Mentoring is a huge part of what we do—throughout the year—at Shawmut. Whether it’s working with our staff internally or reaching out to community groups, we’re committed to cultivating new talent in the construction field. Though we could list many Shawmut Retail mentors, we’d like to give special recognition to three exceptional ones.
  JON FIATO, Senior Project Manager, Mentor for 3 years. Named our “Coach of the Year” for his mentoring efforts at Shawmut. Most recent project: Van Cleef & Arpels, New York
  MARK PRESSON, Senior Estimator, Mentor for 12 years. Most recent project: Apple, Rockingham Park, NH
  BOB GOEMAAT, Construction Manager, Mentor for 7 years. Most recent project: Harman, New York
